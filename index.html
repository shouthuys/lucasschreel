Amery

I have the opportunity to work occasionaly with a Belgian pop-artist called Amery. Catchy summer tunes inspired by Michael Jackson, James Brown and Bruno Mars. A versatile job for me with electric guitar, acoustic guitar, keyboards and Roland SPD-s. An upcoming pop god that you should keep an eye on!
